# Tutorial: Make Your First Pot of Tea

<!-- Quadrant: Tutorial. Source: https://diataxis.fr/tutorials/ -->

This tutorial guides you through brewing your first pot of tea. By the end, you have a hot pot ready to pour.

## Before you start

You need:

- A kettle.
- A teapot (any size).
- Loose-leaf black tea — one teaspoon per cup you plan to make.
- Tap water.

## Step 1: Boil water

Fill the kettle with fresh cold water. Turn it on. Wait until it clicks off.

You should hear the click and see steam rising from the spout.

## Step 2: Warm the pot

Pour a small amount of the boiling water into the empty teapot. Swirl it around. Pour it out.

The pot is now warm to the touch.

## Step 3: Add the tea

Add one teaspoon of tea leaves per cup to the warm pot.

## Step 4: Fill the pot

Pour the rest of the boiling water into the pot over the leaves. Put the lid on.

## Step 5: Wait

Let the pot sit for 4 minutes. Do not stir.

After 4 minutes, the tea is brewed. Pour a cup through a strainer and drink.

## What you made

A pot of black tea, brewed at a standard strength. You can adjust the leaf quantity and steep time next time to taste.
